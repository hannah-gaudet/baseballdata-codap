Getting Started

This workspace contains four tables containing baseball statistics from 2019. The FanGraphs Hitter Leaderboard table should be open.

We're going to work with this dataset to learn the following skills:

  1. Explain the utility of math and data science in the Phillies organization and/or outside of school
  2. Recognize common baseball statistics
  3. Describe simple summary statistics and their relationship to graphs
