How can I look for patterns?

Graphs are wonderful for noticing patterns – much better than a list of numbers!
  • When you want to compare two variables, make a new graph with one variable on each axis.
  • Focus on the shape of the graph first. How would you describe the relationship in words? Are there any points that seem out of place?
  • Do more runs to fill in gaps and add extreme values.
  • See if the results are variable or erratic by comparing two identical runs.
  • Connect points using the ruler menu.
  • Compare the shape to a movable line using the ruler menu.
